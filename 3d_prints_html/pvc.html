Michael Vladimirsky

Edge banding can be an incredibly messy process if you don't have the right tools to manage the adhesive. I built this glue applicator to handle the adhesive distribution more cleanly and evenly than a traditional brush ever could. It allows me to apply a consistent, controlled bead of glue along the edge of plywood, which makes the final iron-on application much smoother and more reliable. It is a simple, shop-made tool, but it is a perfect example of identifying a repetitive shop bottleneck and engineering a quick, effective solution to solve it, saving time in the long run.
