This is one of my favorite projects—I wanted to bridge the gap between a standard keyboard and a flight simulation controller. I integrated actual analog joysticks into a custom mechanical keyboard layout to give me more control over complex macro operations during intense workflows. This project required designing a custom PCB, managing the wiring matrix for the mechanical switches, and modeling a rigid enclosure that wouldn't flex under aggressive use. It might be an overkill solution for most tasks, but for my specific software needs, it is an absolute powerhouse for workflow efficiency.
