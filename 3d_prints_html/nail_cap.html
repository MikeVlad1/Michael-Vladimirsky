Michael Vladimirsky

In my woodshop, I noticed my nail gun was frequently leaving tiny, annoying pressure marks on the surface of softer hardwoods, which meant I had to spend extra time sanding or filling. I designed this protective cap to create a 'soft-touch' interface that absorbs the force of the tool against the wood. By using a slightly flexible filament, the cap conforms to the material, providing a non-slip grip that significantly reduces finish damage. It was a quick engineering win that has already saved me hours of post-processing work and improved the overall quality of my finished projects.
