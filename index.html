Michael Vladimirsky

Hi, I’m an electrical engineer! This basically means I’ve spent an unreasonable amount of time thinking about wires, signals, and why things randomly stop working five minutes before a deadline. I’m fascinated by how tiny components and smart code can come together to create something that moves, senses, or responds. Whether I’m prototyping a circuit, debugging firmware at 2 AM with snacks and desperation, or figuring out how to power something without frying it (again), I genuinely enjoy solving real world problems. My favorite projects are the ones where hardware meets humans like assistive tech, robotics, or bioelectronic systems. I believe tech should feel useful, seamless, and just a little magical. Also, multimeters are my emotional support tools.
