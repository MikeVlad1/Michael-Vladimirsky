Michael Vladimirsky

This automatic waterer is a compact, user-friendly system built around an Arduino Uno R3. It uses a Gikfun 3V-5V submersible water pump controlled through an IRF9540 P-channel MOSFET and NPN transistors, powered by a 9V battery. A 2x16 LCD (shown upside down in the photo-oops!) displays the settings clearly, while a few buttons with custom 3D printed caps make scheduling easy and intuitive. Users can program specific days for watering, making it a hassle-free way to keep plants healthy and hydrated, even when you're not around.
